# Project: Full Partners Modernization

## Design Context

### Users
Logistics directors, operating partners, warehouse managers and supervisors. They need efficiency, clarity, and proof of reliability for complex operations.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Authoritative yet innovative.
- **Tone**: Precision-oriented, transparent, and sophisticated.
- **3-Word Personality**: Professionalism, Trust, Innovation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Modern Industrial Luxury (Architectural Flow).
- **Theme**: Light mode (Clean, high-performance feel).
- **Style**: Avoid static or flat components—use depth, motion, and refined layering.
- **References**: Industrial-grade precision, architectural diagrams.
- **Anti-References**: Generic "safe" corporate designs, flat grids without rhythm, static illustrations.

### Design Principles
1. **Engineered Precision**: Every pixel, space, and transition must feel intentional and structural.
2. **Dynamic Depth**: Leverage tonal shifts (`--surface-...`) and micro-animations to avoid a "flat" feel.
3. **Information Clarity**: Design for scanning. Logistics professionals need to find information quickly but with a premium experience.
4. **Motion with Purpose**: Transitions should reinforce the "flow" of logistics (staggered entries, smooth deceleration).

---

## Technical Quality Standards

- **Typography**: Inter (High Impact/Display), Manrope (UI/Labels). No generic fonts.
- **Performance**: High frame rate GSAP animations, optimized assets, fluid layouts (`clamp`).
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA standards, clear focus states, semantic landmarks.
